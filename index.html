How to read this report

This dashboard shows, in one place, how people find Sicora online, what they do on your website, and how that marketing turns into leads and clients. Each numbered section below covers one part of that story, and every section starts with a short “What this shows” note in plain English.

Use the dropdowns at the very top to choose a time period (e.g. Last 3 Months) and what to look at — the whole report updates automatically. Green numbers are improvements versus the comparison period; red are declines. The Executive Summary just below is a plain-language written read of the most important takeaways and what to do next.

Sessions = website visits Key events / conversions = valuable actions like a form fill Organic = free Google results Paid = Google Ads Pipeline = deals in progress
